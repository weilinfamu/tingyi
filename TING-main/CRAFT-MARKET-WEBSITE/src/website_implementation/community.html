Strength Exercises

WHY

Improves mobility and independence. Helps us do what we want in life. Getting up from a chair with ease, going up and down stairs, and walking outside with confidence all require good strength.

HOW

Standing up and sitting down several times in a row, or going up and down an extra flight of stairs can help. Some people use leg and arm weights to improve strength at a gym or at home.

WHEN

Aim to do strengthening exercises 2-3 times per week.
